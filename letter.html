For You

I just wanted to say how much I appreciate you.

Not just for the big things,
but for the small, quiet ones too.

The way you support me even when I doubt myself.
The way you make me laugh when I least expect it.
The way you listen — really listen.

The way you stay.

You make ordinary days feel special.
You make stressful days feel lighter.

With you, things feel safe.
Easy. Real.

Thank you for being exactly who you are.

I’m really lucky to have you.

I hope you always remember —
you are deeply appreciated,
genuinely admired,
and very, very loved.

Happy Valentine’s Day ❤️
